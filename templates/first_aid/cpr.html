CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation) Guide

CPR Demonstration Video:



Steps for Performing CPR:

  1. Check Responsiveness: Tap the person and ask loudly, "Are you okay?"
  2. Call for Emergency Help: Dial the emergency number (e.g., 911 or 108) immediately.
  3. Open the Airway: Tilt the person's head back slightly and lift the chin.
  4. Check for Breathing: Look, listen, and feel for breathing for no more than 10 seconds.
  5. Start Chest Compressions:
    • Place your hands on the center of the chest.
    • Push down hard and fast (about 2 inches deep).
    • Perform compressions at a rate of 100-120 per minute.
  6. Give Rescue Breaths: If trained, give 2 rescue breaths after every 30 compressions.
  7. Continue CPR: Keep performing CPR until professional help arrives.
